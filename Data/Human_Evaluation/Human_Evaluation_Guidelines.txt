                            Human Evaluation Guidelines
1. Purpose of this Evaluation

The purpose of this evaluation step is in assessing the quality of AI-adapted texts in comparison to
the original texts.
Evaluators will compare an original texts with its adapted version and assess the adaptations on 
the following three dimensions:
    1. Voice Preservation
    2. Meaning Preservation
    3. Accessability
Each dimension will be scored on a scale of 1 to 5

2. Evaluation Procedure
For each evaluated text:
    1. First, read the original text carefully
    2. Read the adapted version
    3. Compare the two texts
    4. Assign a score to each evaluation dimension
    5. Provide a short comment explaining the scores

3. Scoring scale
The following scale will be used for all scores:
Score     |    Description
==========================
1         | Very Poor
2         | Poor
3         | Acceptable
4         | Good
5         | Excellent

4. Definitions used for Evaluation
4.1 Voice Preservation
- Definition: Voice preservation measures how well the adapted text maintains the style, tone,
and perspective of the original curatorial text
- What to consider for the voice dimension:
    1. Is the tone similar?
    2. Is the interpretative perspective maintained?
    3. Does the text still sound professional?
    4. Has the authors intent been preserved?
4.2 Meaning Preservation
- Definition: Meaning preservation measures how much of the original context is present
in the adapted version
- What to consider for the meaning dimension:
    1. Are the main ideas preserved?
    2. Are the important facts retained?
    3. Has any information been added or removed?
    4. Are there any distorisions?
4.3 Accessability
- Definition: Accessability measures how easy the adapted texts are for each of 
the different strategies
- What to consider for the accessability dimension:
    1. Is the language easier to understand?
    2. Are difficult terms explained or simplified?
    3. Is the sentence structure clear?
    4. Would the target audience understand the text more easily?
